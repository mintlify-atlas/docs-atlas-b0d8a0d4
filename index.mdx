## Learning path

Work through the topics in order for the best understanding, or jump directly to the concept you want to explore.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with LLM fundamentals">
    Get a high-level overview of what LLMs are, and the key concepts around them — RAG, MCP, Agents, fine-tuning, and quantization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand tokenization">
    Learn how text is converted to tokens, and how the Byte Pair Encoding (BPE) algorithm builds a vocabulary from raw text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Master the attention mechanism">
    Work through the math behind Q, K, V attention — including the scaling factor and causal masking used in autoregressive models.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decode the Transformer architecture">
    Understand how tokenization, embeddings, attention, feed-forward networks, and residual connections combine into a full Transformer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore inference optimization">
    Study the techniques that make LLM inference fast and memory-efficient: KV Cache, Paged Attention, Flash Attention, and MoE.
  </Step>
</Steps>
